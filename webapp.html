Here are some links to help you learn how to program. Always use the internet and youtube for more help!

Leetcode is a great way to hone your skills by solving problems. If you're stuck, other people always post their answers, so check them out!

geeksforgeeks is another great website, and can answer any of your coding questions. It might take a while to find the answer, but it'll be there.

stackoverflow is a peer to peer website, where people ask and answer questions. If you're stuck on something that's never been asked before, feel free to ask! Make sure to look up your own question first, however. No one wants to see the same questions every day.
